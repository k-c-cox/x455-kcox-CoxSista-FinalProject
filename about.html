Welcome To CoxSista Adventures

Wondering who the heck we are? Hello ðŸ‘‹ we're Cameron and Kelsey, two sisters who love to travel. We've been traveling together for awhile...over 30 years...but it wasn't until 2016 when CoxSista Adventures was born.

It all started with a wedding invitation. In 2016 Kelsey began to plan to attend her friend Antonia's wedding in Glasgow, Scotland, and to take some time to explore more of Scotland while there. Research done, plane tickets booked, Cameron started to count her vacation days and wondered if she should be booking tickets too. About three weeks before the departure date, she did, making the trip the first of the CoxSista Adventures. Since then, we've gone on many adventures together, from Thailand and Japan to places a little closer to home, like Hawaii.

Who's Cameron?

Cameron is an Interior Designer by trade and passionate about good food, art, and exploring new places. She's the elder of our sister duo who's always down to go out and do something. You'll often find her taking strolls with her Goldendoodle Mackie, binging a new show, and asking: "who designed this?" when confronted with questionable designs.

Who's Kelsey?

Kelsey is a boba-obsessed, doting dog aunt who loves to explore new restaurants, bookstores, museums, and craft fairs. You'll often find her curled up with a good book, in the kitchen tackling new baking projects during her free time, and creating extensive matrixes while planning the next trip!
