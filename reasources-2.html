10 Best Places to Visit in Japan

With its enormous cities and towering skyscrapers brightly clad in neon lights, Japan is a fun country to visit, full of history and with a rich cultural heritage.

Temples and shrines dot its streets, while world-class restaurants rub shoulders with ancient castles and imperial palaces, and its cities bustle with life and energy. Japan is a warm, welcoming and endlessly interesting travel destination with something for everyone. Here's a look at the best places to visit in Japan.

A Two Day Trip To Naoshima

It's been a dream of ours to visit Naoshima for a few years now, and finally we found an opportunity to work a visit into our last trip to Japan! Naoshima (直島) is an island in the Seto Inland Sea known for its modern art museums, architecture and sculptures.

We had such a memorable two days, and the slightly warmer weather made a lovely change to the sub zero temperatures of Mt Fuji! The daffodils were in bloom and the island was starting to wake up for Spring, so riding our bikes round the island was so enjoyable!

Head over to my blog for more information on where we went, where we stayed and how we got to both Naoshima and Teshima from Mt Fuji (I’ve also included routes for those travelling from Tokyo or Kyoto)! For more info on exactly where we visited check out my blog guide here: https://bit.ly/2m1zyuI
